TESTING ON LPCXPRESSO18S37
==========================

1. Remove the Macronix part from the board and replace with a Gigabyte GB25Qxxx.

2. Close jumpers JP3 and JP5 to ensure ISP booting from USART0

3. Move JP11 to the P2.0 position

4. Remove the DFUlink jumper

5. Connect an RS232 to USB interface to P4 FTDI connector (ground, tx and rx only needed)

6. Press and release SW3 (reset) and SW2 (ISP) to boot into USART0 bootloader

7. Choose bootloader in Flash Magic and select FTDI COM port
